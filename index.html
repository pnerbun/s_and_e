Our Story

A New Home for Great Hair

Smith + Elliot is a boutique hair salon and stylist collective opening this fall in the heart of downtown Delafield — a lovingly restored historic building where a small group of independent stylists share one belief: great hair happens when the people behind the chair love what they do and own how they do it.

It's a space designed to feel timeless: warm, unhurried, and genuinely welcoming — whether you're settling in for a fresh style or building your own business behind one of our chairs.
