# Google Business Profile — Smith + Elliot

The single highest-impact local SEO asset for this client. The website supports the listing;
for "hair salon Delafield WI" the **GBP listing is what actually wins the map pack.**

## Business description (745 / 750 chars — paste as-is)

```
Smith + Elliot is a boutique hair salon and stylist collective in historic downtown Delafield, Wisconsin. Set in a restored 1800s cream city brick building on Genesee Street, we offer women's and men's haircuts, single-process color, foils, balayage, toners, blowouts, and deep conditioning treatments.

We're built differently: our stylists are independent professionals who own their business and set their own menu, so the person in your chair is genuinely invested in your hair. Every service is unhurried and tailored to you, in a space designed to feel timeless and welcoming.

We serve Delafield, Oconomowoc, Hartland, Pewaukee, Waukesha and Lake Country. Chairs are also available for licensed cosmetologists building their own business.
```

**Why it's written this way**
- **745/750 chars.** Google truncates at 750 — no room to pad.
- **Front-loaded.** Only ~250 chars show before "more", so the first sentence carries the
  primary keyword (*hair salon*), the city (*Delafield*), and the differentiator (*collective*).
- **Policy-safe.** GBP descriptions may not contain URLs, prices, promotions/offers, or ALL CAPS.
  That's why there are no dollar figures (they live on `services.html`) and no "Now Leasing!".
- Keywords appear naturally: hair salon · Delafield · balayage · color · haircuts · stylist ·
  Lake Country. No stuffing — Google penalizes it and it reads badly to humans.
- The chair-rental line is last: it's real business info, but clients are the primary audience.

### ⚠️ NAP consistency

Name / Address / Phone must match the website **character for character** — it's a direct local
ranking factor. The site currently uses **"715 Genesee Street"** spelled out. If GBP normalizes
it to **"715 Genesee St"**, change the site to match (one find/replace across the HTML + the
`HairSalon` JSON-LD on `index.html` and `contact.html`).

### Photos

The listing needs photos, and GBP rewards real ones. Currently available: the building exterior
and the sign. The interior shots are **renders, not the actual space** — do not upload them as
if they were real. Shoot the finished interior before launch; add the logo and cover photo too.
