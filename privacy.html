Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Smith + Elliot ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit our website or contact us, and how we use it.

Information We Collect

Our website does not require you to create an account or submit a form. If you choose to contact us by phone, text, or email, we receive the information you share — such as your name, phone number, email address, and the details of your inquiry — so that we can respond and, if you wish, schedule an appointment.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your questions and booking requests
  • To schedule, confirm, and follow up on appointments
  • To communicate about our services and, only if you opt in, occasional updates

We do not sell or rent your personal information to anyone.

Cookies & Analytics

This site is a simple static website. We may use privacy-friendly analytics to understand general traffic patterns, and third-party services such as an embedded map (Google Maps) may set their own cookies. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Third-Party Links

Our site links to third-party platforms such as Instagram, Facebook, and Google Maps. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies, and we encourage you to review them.

Independent Stylists

Smith + Elliot is a collective of independent stylists. Each stylist operates their own business and may collect and handle client information separately. Booking directly with an individual stylist is subject to their own practices.
